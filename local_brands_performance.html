Commission Framework
Flat 20% + Two Structural Rules
Simple to communicate · Fair to the platform · Accountability built in for underperformers

How commission works
Al Rugaib collects 100% of the customer payment (product + delivery). Commission is deducted at source before vendor settlement. Calculated on the final customer-paid price including delivery, net of confirmed returns. One monthly settlement cycle, itemized statement issued 5 days prior.
Rule 1 — Delivery Fee Deduction
Applies only where Al Rugaib handles last-mile delivery (Model B). Logistics cost recovery deducted from net settlement before 20% commission is applied.

SAR 25/order — items ≤ 70×70×80 cm
SAR 40/order — items above threshold
Rule 2 — Return Rate Penalty
Applies to any vendor whose return rate breaches the threshold. Automatic — no manual intervention.

Trigger: RR > 7% for 60 consecutive days
Penalty: Commission steps to 23%
Exit: Reverts to 20% when RR < 5% for 30 days
